Augustine, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Prague

Located in the historic heart of Prague’s Lesser Town (Malá Strana), Augustine, a Luxury Collection Hotel is a five-star sanctuary steeped in history, elegance, and monastic calm. Part of Marriott’s prestigious Luxury Collection, this hotel offers an unforgettable blend of heritage, refinement, and world-class hospitality—perfect for travelers seeking both authenticity and luxury.

Historical Setting: A Monastery Turned Masterpiece

What sets Augustine apart is its unique location within a 13th-century Augustine monastery, parts of which are still inhabited by practicing monks. The hotel complex integrates seven historic buildings, including the St. Thomas Monastery, creating an atmosphere that is deeply tranquil, spiritual, and architecturally magnificent.

Guests will find cloistered courtyards, frescoed ceilings, and even rooms that overlook the monastery’s private chapel and gardens. You’re not just staying in Prague—you’re living its history.

Rooms & Suites: Quiet Luxury With Czech Character

  • Room Types:

  • Deluxe Rooms

  • Premium Garden View Rooms

  • Junior Suites

  • Signature Suites (incl. Fresco Suite and Tower Suite)

The rooms are designed with a monastic aesthetic: understated yet luxurious, with vaulted ceilings, arched windows, and custom-made Czech furnishings. Bathrooms are spacious, with heated floors, marble finishings, and deep soaking tubs. High-end touches like Etro toiletries, plush bedding, and state-of-the-art soundproofing ensure restful comfort.

The Tower Suite, a standout feature, is set over three floors in a former observatory, offering panoramic views of Prague Castle and the Vltava River.

Dining: Refined Local Flavors & Rare Beers

  • Augustine Restaurant

Overseen by Executive Chef Martin Boháček, the restaurant combines modern Czech cuisine with international elegance. Expect seasonal menus with dishes like duck confit, beef sirloin in creamy dill sauce, and forest mushroom risotto. Ingredients are sourced from local farms and crafted with minimal fuss for maximum flavor.

  • Refectory Bar 1887

Set in a dramatic vaulted hall with original frescoes, the bar serves craft cocktails alongside a rare treat: St. Thomas Beer , brewed using a secret recipe by the resident monks. It’s the only place in the world you can taste it.

Wellness & Amenities

  • Spa: A boutique wellness suite offering massages, facials, and Ayurvedic treatments.

  • Fitness Room: 24-hour access with Technogym equipment.

  • Secret Garden: A private cloister garden ideal for peaceful reading or al fresco tea.

  • Chauffeur Service: Mercedes-Benz transfers available.

  • Concierge: Expert staff ready to arrange private castle tours, opera tickets, or riverside dining.

Location: Quiet Yet Central

  • 5-minute walk to Charles Bridge

  • 10-minute walk to Prague Castle

  • Tucked away from tourist noise but within walking distance of major landmarks

You’re in one of the most picturesque parts of Prague, surrounded by cobblestone streets, baroque churches, and charming cafés.

Final Verdict

Augustine, a Luxury Collection Hotel is not just a hotel—it’s an experience. Perfect for honeymooners, cultural travelers, and luxury seekers, it offers a tranquil oasis in one of Europe’s most vibrant cities. Its fusion of historical reverence and contemporary comfort makes it a standout property not just in Prague, but across Central Europe.
