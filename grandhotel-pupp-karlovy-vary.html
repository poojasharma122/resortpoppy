Grandhotel Pupp, Karlovy Vary

With over three centuries of history and a guest list that includes royalty, artists, and film stars, Grandhotel Pupp is not just a hotel—it’s a legend. Set along the Teplá River in the spa town of Karlovy Vary, this five-star property is a Czech national treasure and a top destination for luxury wellness, classical architecture, and cinematic flair.

Historical Significance & Architecture

Founded in 1701, Grandhotel Pupp has evolved from a family-run inn into one of Europe’s most iconic hotels. Its Neo-Baroque façade, grand ballrooms, and ornate interiors recall the height of Habsburg elegance. The hotel gained international fame as the inspiration for the "Hotel Splendide" in Casino Royale and featured prominently in The Grand Budapest Hotel.

The atmosphere is regal but not pretentious, blending old-world glamour with modern refinement.

Rooms & Suites: Elegance with a Heritage Touch

Accommodation Types:

  • Standard & Superior Rooms (Pupp Wing or Park Wing)

  • Junior Suites

  • Grand Suites

  • Presidential Suite

Each room is uniquely decorated with high ceilings, chandeliers, period furnishings, and classic Czech textiles. Bathrooms are finished in marble, with heated floors and luxury toiletries.

The Presidential Suite includes views of the river promenade, a separate living area, and antique décor from the 19th century. Most rooms overlook Dvořák Park, the river, or the colonnades.

Spa & Wellness: World-Class Hydrotherapy

At the heart of the Pupp experience is its extensive spa and wellness center, where guests can enjoy both traditional Czech treatments and international therapies.

Spa Features:

  • 15-meter indoor relaxation pool

  • Finnish sauna, herbal sauna, and steam bath

  • Hydrotherapy baths with local mineral water

  • Massages, facials, and detox ritua

  • Cryotherapy and lymphatic drainage options

Dining: Old-World Service & Modern Cuisine

  • Grandrestaurant Pupp

A classic fine-dining experience in a hall that recalls the Austro-Hungarian empire, complete with crystal chandeliers and live piano music. The menu combines Czech gastronomy with European finesse, such as venison ragout, creamy svíčková, and handmade spa wafers.

  • Café Pupp

Famous for its Pupp Cake, the café serves artisanal desserts, traditional Czech pastries, and light lunch dishes. Elegant and relaxed, with river views.

  • Becher’s Bar

An intimate cocktail bar with Art Deco interiors. Excellent for a nightcap, jazz evenings, or sampling the town’s famous herbal liqueur, Becherovka.

Activities & Experiences

  • Attend a concert at the Karlovy Vary International Film Festival, hosted at Pupp

  • Book a classical carriage ride through the town

  • Explore the nearby Mill Colonnade, hot springs, and Diana Tower

  • Golf packages available at nearby Golf Resort Karlovy Vary

  • Luxury shopping arcade within the hotel

Location: The Jewel of West Bohemia

  • Located at the western edge of Karlovy Vary’s spa promena

  • Overlooks the Teplá River and Dvořák Park

  • Walking distance to springs, colonnades, and forest trails

It’s a quiet, picturesque setting ideal for wellness, romance, or literary inspiration.

Guest Experience & Ratings

Average Guest Rating: ★★★★★ (4.8/5)

Pros:

  • Rich cultural history and architectural beauty

  • World-class spa facilitie

  • Impeccable service with multilingual staff

  • Superb dining and classic café atmosphere

  • Exclusive, peaceful location

Cons:

  • Some rooms in the Park Wing are more traditional and may feel dated to minimalists

  • Peak seasons (e.g. film festival) can result in limited availability and higher prices

Final Verdict

Grandhotel Pupp delivers an unmatched combination of timeless luxury, wellness, and Bohemian culture. Perfect for romantic getaways, spa retreats, or grand European tours, it is a must-visit for anyone who wants to immerse themselves in Czech elegance and cinematic history.
