Hotel Esplanade Spa & Golf Resort, Mariánské Lázně

Perched on a hill overlooking the elegant spa town of Mariánské Lázně, the Hotel Esplanade Spa & Golf Resort is a jewel of Belle Époque luxury in the heart of the Czech Republic’s famed spa region. Built in 1911 for the aristocracy of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, the hotel remains a sanctuary of wellness, heritage, and refined leisure.

Historic Grandeur & Panoramic Views

The hotel’s pink-and-white façade and wrought-iron balconies immediately signal classic luxury. Inside, guests are greeted with marble floors, grand staircases, and chandeliers— a nod to the grandeur of another era.

From its elevated position, the Esplanade offers sweeping views of Mariánské Lázně and the surrounding forests. The setting is peaceful yet just a short stroll from the colonnade and singing fountain.

Rooms & Suites: Classic Elegance and Spa

Accommodation Types:

  • Superior and Deluxe Rooms

  • Junior Suites

  • Presidential and Royal Suites

Rooms are spacious, ranging from 30–120 m², with high ceilings, period furniture, and large windows offering town or park views. Beds are ultra-comfortable with high-thread-count linens, and each room includes a work desk, lounge chairs, and a minibar.

Bathrooms feature marble fittings, heated towel racks, and luxury amenities from L’Occitane.

Spa & Wellness: Healing in Tradition

The Esplanade’s medical-grade spa and wellness center is one of the finest in Czechia, focusing on therapeutic treatments using natural mineral springs, peat, and gas treatments unique to the region.

Offerings:

  • Traditional balneotherapy

  • Dry carbon dioxide baths

  • Massages and physiotherapy

  • Infrared saunas and whirlpools

  • Personal wellness programs (7–21 days)

Medical staff is on-site for wellness diagnostics and curated spa regimens. This is a true spa hotel, not just a luxury property with a wellness room.

Dining: Elegant European Cuisine

  • King Edward VII Restaurant

Named after the British monarch and frequent spa visitor, this fine dining room serves a mix of Czech specialties and continental classics, with white-glove service and an old-world ambiance.

Signature dishes include:

  • Venison loin with cranberry jus

  • Cream of mushroom soup with local truffles

  • Classic Czech svíčková (beef with root vegetable cream sauce)

  • Lobby Bar & Summer Terrac

Ideal for cocktails, afternoon tea, or light snacks with panoramic views of the valley.

Leisure & Activities

  • 18-hole Royal Golf Club Mariánské Lázně (5 minutes away)

  • On-site tennis courts and fitness room

  • Walking trails to forest viewpoints

  • Cultural events, piano concerts, and spa town festivals year-round

Guests can also enjoy bespoke packages such as "Golf & Spa Getaways" or "Healing Weeks" , combining relaxation and recreation.

Location: Wellness Capital of West Bohemia

  • 800 meters from the Colonnade and Singing Fountain

  • Nearby attractions: Boheminium Park, Forest Spring, and spa promenades

  • Direct access to forest hiking trails

Mariánské Lázně is known for its mild climate, clean air, and healing waters — perfect for a wellness-focused escape.

Guest Experience & Ratings

Average Guest Rating: ★★★★★ (4.7/5)

Pros:

  • Spacious rooms with historic charm

  • Top-tier medical spa and wellness facilities

  • Quiet location with spectacular views

  • Proximity to golf and nature

  • Impeccable dining and service

Cons:

  • Traditional design may feel old-fashioned to modern minimalists

  • Limited nightlife or entertainment options on-site

Final Verdict

The Hotel Esplanade Spa & Golf Resort is ideal for travelers seeking restoration, peace, and aristocratic charm. It’s not just a hotel—it’s an immersive wellness retreat. Whether you’re planning a week-long spa therapy, a romantic getaway, or a luxury golf break, the Esplanade is a refined and rejuvenating choice.
