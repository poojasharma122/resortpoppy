About Us

Welcome to ResortPoppy.com — your trusted guide to luxury hotel experiences across Czechia.

We specialize in curating and comparing the finest five-star resorts, boutique stays, castle hotels, and spa retreats, helping discerning travelers book with confidence, clarity, and style. Whether you’re dreaming of a riverside suite in Prague, a royal spa weekend in Karlovy Vary, or a romantic vineyard escape in Moravia, we bring Czechia’s best accommodations to one simple, beautiful platform.

Our Story

ResortPoppy.com began with a single vision:

To showcase the elegance and diversity of Czech luxury hospitality to the world.

Inspired by the serenity of Bohemian spa towns and the rich architectural heritage of Prague and beyond, we set out to build a platform that reflects the soul of the country—while making travel planning effortless for our users.
